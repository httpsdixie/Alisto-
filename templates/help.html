Help & Frequently Asked Questions

About Alisto!

Alisto! is the official campus safety reporting system for Eastern Visayas State University - Ormoc Campus (EVSU-OC). It allows students and faculty to report safety concerns and maintenance issues in real-time, ensuring a safer campus environment for everyone.

  1. Log in to your account (or register if you don't have one)
  2. Click "New Report" in the navigation menu
  3. Fill out the report form with details about the issue
  4. Optionally add a photo as evidence
  5. Select the appropriate category and priority level
  6. Click "Submit Report"

You'll receive a unique ticket ID to track your report's progress.

There are two ways to track your report:

  • If logged in: Go to "My Reports" to see all your submissions and their current status.
  • Using Ticket ID: Use the "Track Report" feature and enter your ticket ID (e.g., ALT-20251202-ABC123).

  • Pending - Your report has been received and is awaiting review.
  • In Progress - The maintenance team is actively working on the issue.
  • Resolved - The issue has been fixed. Please provide feedback!

  • Infrastructure - Building damage, broken facilities, structural issues
  • Safety Hazard - Dangerous conditions, obstacles, tripping hazards
  • Electrical - Broken lights, exposed wires, outlet issues
  • Plumbing - Leaks, clogged drains, water pressure problems
  • Sanitation - Cleanliness issues, waste disposal, pest control
  • Security - Broken locks, unauthorized access, safety concerns
  • Other - Any issue that doesn't fit the above categories

When submitting a new report, click the QR code icon next to the location field. This will open your device's camera. Point it at a location QR code posted around campus, and the location will be automatically filled in.

To prevent spam and ensure quality reports, there's a limit of 5 reports per user per 24 hours. Additionally, you cannot submit duplicate reports (same title and location) within 24 hours.

  1. Click "Forgot password?" on the login page
  2. Enter your registered email address
  3. Check your email for a password reset link
  4. Click the link and create a new password

Note: The reset link expires after 1 hour for security reasons.
