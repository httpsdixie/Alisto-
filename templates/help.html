Help & Support

  1. Click on "New Report" in the navigation menu
  2. Fill in the report title with a brief description of the issue
  3. Enter the specific location of the problem
  4. Provide a detailed description (at least 10 characters)
  5. Select the appropriate category and priority level
  6. Optionally upload a photo as evidence (max 5MB)
  7. Click "Submit Report"

You'll receive a Ticket ID after submission - save this for tracking!

There are two ways to track your reports:

  • Dashboard: View all your recent reports directly on your dashboard
  • Track Report: Use the "Track Report" feature and enter your Ticket ID

You'll also receive email notifications when your report status changes.

  • Pending Report has been submitted and is awaiting review by the maintenance team
  • In Progress The maintenance team is actively working on resolving the issue
  • Resolved The issue has been fixed. You can now provide feedback!

Once your report is marked as Resolved:

  1. Go to the report details page
  2. You'll see a feedback form at the bottom
  3. Rate your experience (1-5 stars)
  4. Add any comments (optional)
  5. Confirm if the issue was actually resolved
  6. Click "Submit Feedback"

Your feedback helps us improve campus safety services!

  • Low Minor issues that don't require immediate attention (e.g., small cosmetic damage)
  • Medium Issues that should be addressed soon but aren't urgent (e.g., broken fixtures)
  • High Urgent safety concerns that need immediate attention (e.g., exposed wiring, flooding)

  • Maximum file size: 5MB
  • Supported formats: JPG, JPEG, PNG, GIF
  • Photos are automatically resized for optimal storage
  • Photos are optional but highly recommended for faster resolution
